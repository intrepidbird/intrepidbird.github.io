About Me


Welcome to my about me! I am Alex Sheng, or you might know me as IntrepidBird. I am currently a student on the US East Coast.

You might be interested in some of my projects or blog entries. If you're here to learn about me, look below to learn about who I am and my various experiences.

Contact me at intrepidbird (Discord) or intrepidman2010@gmail.com.

Passions

I am a math lover who has succeeded and won awards in prestigious math competitions like the AMC series, AIME, ARML, MathCounts, CML, Math League, and others. I also enjoy science and have won tournaments like the Logistem Science Challeger, the Science Bowl, and others. I'm also a 2x hackathon winner, an informatics tournament top scorer, a cybersecurity competition winner, and I create coding projects available to the public on my web domain and open source platforms. My interests within computer science lie mostly in web development, but I also write lots of mathematical and scientific Python code and am interested in robotics. Besides all my STEM interests, I also enjoy reading nonfiction, hanging out with friends, playing ice hockey, tennis, table tennis, band instruments, and watching logic-based and sci-fi movies.

Mathematics

My primary passion is mathematics, where I captain my school's Math Competition Team (4th in Chapter), am a 3x AMC 8 honor roll achiever, was in the top 10 in my chapter for MathCounts, was #1 in my region with my team for CML, achieved a perfect score on the Math League competition, and am a 2x Gauss Math Tournament top scorer. My favorite subjects in math are algebra and combinatorics, as algebra seems like the most logical of all the mathematical disciplines and combinatorics is often amongst the most difficult. I am also involved on Art of Problem Solving, a math forum, where I host a blog and occasionally play AoPS For The Win (FTW). I'm particularly interested in participating in high school math competitions next year and hoping to suceed in them (I am a rising freshman). I also plan to be an intern for LIVE by Po-Shen Loh, the former IMO head coach, next year (or maybe even this year). I serve as the founder of IntrepidMaths, which is a group of passionate and eager mathematicians who engage in enriching math discussions on Discord.
